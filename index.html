Fundeni Lake
Protected Area

Located in Bucharest, Romania, Fundeni Lake is part of Colentina Lakes Chain , a series of anthropic lakes formed on Colentina River, conceived as a northern green belt of the city.

The lake with its shores, a green urban area with remarkable natural value and biodiversity, a habitat for many wild species, is proposed to become a Protected Area.

Reference: Colentina Declaration

1.

Long term sustainable policy vision.

Goal

Develop coherent approaches to:

  • enhancing mobility
  • increase access to the natural environment
  • provide social and cultural opportunities and employment

2.

Coherent planning concepts for the periphery.

Goal

Maintain a healthy connections between the urban and rural landscape.

3.

Unified administrative approach to the lakes chain of the local, municipal and sectoral authorities.

Goal

Formalize a single strategy and neutral governing entity (nonprofit organization or development authority).

4.

Connection infrastructure for cycling and hiking to the forested area to the east of the lakes.

Goal

Enhance the site's potential and connectivity.
